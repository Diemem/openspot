# Landlord Video Guide - Explore Screen

## How to Create Effective Property Videos

### Video Specifications

#### Technical Requirements
- **Format**: MP4, MOV, or WebM
- **Max Size**: 100MB
- **Max Duration**: 60 seconds
- **Recommended Resolution**: 720p (1280×720)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical/portrait)
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps minimum

#### Quality Guidelines
- Good lighting (natural light preferred)
- Stable footage (use tripod or stabilizer)
- Clear audio (if narrating)
- No watermarks or logos (unless your brand)

### Content Structure (60 seconds)

#### Opening (0-5 seconds)
- **Hook**: Eye-catching exterior shot or best feature
- **Text Overlay**: Property type and price
- Example: "2BR Apartment - KES 25,000/month"

#### Tour (5-45 seconds)
- **Living Room**: 5-8 seconds
- **Kitchen**: 5-8 seconds
- **Bedrooms**: 8-12 seconds each
- **Bathroom**: 5-8 seconds
- **Balcony/Outdoor**: 5-8 seconds (if applicable)

#### Closing (45-60 seconds)
- **Amenities**: Quick shots of parking, security, water
- **Location**: Nearby landmarks (university, mall, hospital)
- **Call-to-Action**: "Contact now" with phone number

### Video Tips

#### DO's ✅
- Show natural light (open curtains)
- Highlight unique features (balcony, view, modern kitchen)
- Include neighborhood shots (nearby shops, transport)
- Add text overlays (price, bedrooms, location)
- Show amenities (parking, security gate, water tank)
- Film during daytime (better lighting)
- Keep camera steady
- Show actual property (no stock footage)

#### DON'Ts ❌
- Don't film in dark rooms
- Don't use shaky handheld footage
- Don't include tenants or personal items
- Don't exaggerate features
- Don't use misleading thumbnails
- Don't add loud music (users might be in public)
- Don't exceed 60 seconds
- Don't upload low-quality videos

### Thumbnail Selection

Your thumbnail is the first thing users see. Choose wisely:

#### Best Thumbnails
- Bright, well-lit exterior shot
- Modern kitchen with appliances
- Spacious living room
- Balcony with view
- Clean, empty bedroom

#### Avoid
- Dark or blurry images
- Cluttered rooms
- Construction/renovation shots
- Unflattering angles

• [Distance] from [Landmark]

### Upload Process

1. **Prepare Video**
   - Export in MP4 format
   - Check file size (<100MB)
   - Verify duration (<60s)

2. **Upload to OpenSpot**
   - Go to "My Properties"
   - Select property
   - Click "Add Video"
   - Upload video file
   - Wait for processing

3. **Add Details**
   - Write compelling description
   - Add relevant hashtags
   - Select thumbnail (auto-generated or custom)
   - Set availability status

4. **Publish**
   - Review preview
   - Click "Publish to Explore"
   - Video goes live after moderation (24-48 hours)

### Optimization Tips

#### If Low Views
- Improve thumbnail (brighter, clearer)
- Add more hashtags
- Post during peak hours (6-9 PM)
- Consider sponsored listing

#### If Low Watch Time
- Hook viewers in first 3 seconds
- Show best features first
- Reduce video length
- Improve lighting/quality

#### If Low Contact Clicks
- Make price more prominent
- Highlight unique features
- Add clear call-to-action
- Verify contact info is correct

### Legal & Ethical Guidelines

#### Must Do
- Show actual property (no fake footage)
- Disclose all fees upfront
- Accurate location information
- Real contact information
- Honest condition assessment

#### Must Not Do
- Misleading pricing
- Fake amenities
- Stock footage/photos
- Discriminatory language
- Harassment of viewers
